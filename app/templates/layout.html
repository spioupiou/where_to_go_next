Where should you go next?


Not sure how to spend your next weekend in Japan? No worries, I've got your back!


Japan is the largest island in the world and odds are that you have not seen everything yet. From the northern lands of Hokkaido to the sub-tropical Okinawa archipelago, many adventures lie ahead.


Please use the select menu if you wish to travel within a specific area, and click on the button to find out your next travel destination. You can also find information and some pictures on the main cities of Japan by clicking on the green city markers.


And if you are not convinced by the city that shows up on the map, you can try to press the button again.
